VOX Amplug


The VOX amplug is a perfect amplifier for quiet practice! This little amplifier plugs into your bass guitar and does not have its own speaker, but is played with headphones through a regular headphone jack. You can adjust tone and volume like on any amplifier and can select from a few drumming patterns to play along to. The VOX amplug is a must-have for any bassists who may play at odd-times or with others and do not wish to disturb them!

Fender Rumble 15


The Fender Rumble 15 is a 15 Watt bass amplifier that is small and portable, but loud and powerful. The fender rumble 15 is considered an amp for practice and not live playing because of its low wattage. This amp is suitable for practice and playing with music that isn’t too loud but probably wouldn’t be very ideal for someone playing live in a rock or metal band. However, it is still powerful and produces a lot of power for something of its size while still having good tone and sound quality. The rumble 15 has a headphone jack that allows for quiet playing and an AUX input for players to play music through the amp to practice or play along to.

Blackstar Fly 3


The blackstar fly 3 is a battery-powered mini amp designed for the bass guitar. This small amp has a 3 inch speaker and can be operated with an extension cabinet for a louder sound. Because this amp is so small, it is only really used for practice and stand-alone playing, or for playing with a guitar, but would not be used in a full band setting because of how quiet it is. The fly 3 has an AUX input, allowing for music to be played through the amp to play along to and for practice, and a standard headphone jack.
