About Us

MUSE 56 is a website that was made to help new musicians and people who are looking to buy a new instrument that is affordable and of good quality. We have researched into the best instruments and manufacturers and collected them to present easily for people who are interested. We specialise in piano and bass guitars, but are available to give advice on a select few other instruments like guitar and drums. If you have any questions or suggestions for us, please feel free to fill out the contact form below! We are always looking to improve and your feedback is extremely helpful to us and is always welcome :)
