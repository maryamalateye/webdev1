Musical instruments are expensive to make and produce, but some companies have lines that are made to not break the bank. For example, the big brand guitar manufacturer Fender also produces guitars and basses under the name Squier. The guitars produced under the Squier name are much cheaper than Fender-name guitars but are good quality and have all the cool designs!


Squier Site!




Similar to how Squier is to Fender, Epiphone exists to Gibson. Epiphone produces guitars and basses that are the same models as Gibson's guitars and basses but at a cheaper price. Epiphone is a good manufacturer for its price point and good quality instruments, and always comes highly recommended.


Epiphone Site!



Thomann is a music shop that is well-known for having a very diverse of products made by all different music manufacturers. They also produce their own instruments and musical equipment that is inexpensive, while still being decent quality. They produce digital pianos, guitars and basses(under the name of Harley Benton), and other musical equipment.
